# 海外与 Remote 求职策略

> 一句话定位：把「想找海外/Remote 工作」拆成可执行的销售漏斗：选路径、改资产、建渠道、跑面试、谈总包、处理合规。
>
> 适合读者：有 5 年以上工程经验，能独立交付复杂系统，希望用 3–6 个月系统性进入国际远程、海外 relocation、或 contractor 市场。

## 0. 学完本章你要交付什么

读完不是「更了解海外求职」，而是产出 5 份可检查的材料：

1. **路径决策表**：明确你优先冲 Remote、relocation、外企在华、还是 contractor，并写出 90 天计划。
2. **目标公司清单**：至少 50 家公司，字段包含地点、时区、薪酬数据、技术栈、内推联系人、申请状态。
3. **英文求职资产**：一页英文简历、LinkedIn headline/about/experience、GitHub/个人站入口。
4. **外联脚本库**：10 封冷启动私信/邮件、3 个 recruiter screen 回答、2 套谈薪邮件。
5. **面试准备看板**：覆盖 coding、system design、behavioral、team match、英语异步沟通。

> 默认你已经能用英文读写技术文档；如果写作费力，先并行训练 [技术写作英语](../english/03-technical-writing-english.md)。

## 1. 路径阶梯：不要把所有「海外机会」混成一类

### 1.1 四条路径的真实差异

| 路径 | 典型合同主体 | 门槛 | 收入上限 | 主要约束 | 推荐优先级 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 国内 → 国际 Remote | 你在国内，服务海外公司 | 中 | 中高到高 | 税务、支付、时区、雇佣合规 | 先试水，最快验证市场 |
| 海外 relocation | 公司 sponsor 签证，你搬到当地 | 高 | 高 | 签证名额、面试强度、家庭迁移 | 中长期主线 |
| 外企在华 / 国际团队 | 中国合同，本地 payroll | 中 | 中高 | HC 波动、全球汇报线 | 过渡与背书 |
| 独立 contractor / fractional | B2B 合同，按小时/月收费 | 高 | 高且波动大 | 获客、收款、税务、无福利 | 有 niche 后放大 |

选择原则：

- **现金流优先**：如果当前收入压力大，先找外企在华或国内可远程外企团队，降低跳跃风险。
- **迁移优先**：如果目标是移民/身份，优先 relocation；纯 Remote 对身份帮助有限。
- **收入优先**：资深工程师可把 Remote 与 contractor 并行跑，目标是用多个 offer/客户提高谈判力。
- **生活方式优先**：Remote 不是少工作，而是更依赖异步输出；不愿写文档、跨时区同步的人会很痛苦。

### 1.2 路径 A：Remote（国内 → 国际远程）

**适合你，如果：**

- 能独立 owner 一个服务/模块，不依赖频繁面对面同步；
- 有 2–4 小时可与欧美或亚太团队 overlap；
- 英文能完成 PR 描述、设计文档、incident update；
- 可以接受合同形式可能是 contractor，而不是当地正式员工。

**优势：**

- 不需要立刻搬家，试错成本低；
- 能快速验证英文简历、面试、薪酬锚点；
- 对后续 relocation 有背书：你已经在国际团队交付过。

**现实约束：**

- 很多公司写 remote，但只限 US/EU/Canada payroll；搜索时要看 `Remote - Worldwide`、`Remote - APAC`、`Anywhere`。
- 国内个人直接收境外服务费涉及外汇、税务、发票/合同问题；不要用「朋友账户代收」这类不可审计方案。
- 美国西海岸团队常要求北京时间 22:00–01:00 overlap；欧洲团队通常更友好，15:00–19:00 可覆盖部分时段。

**90 天目标：**拿到 2–3 个 recruiter screen，至少 1 个 onsite/technical loop，不一定马上跳。

### 1.3 路径 B：海外 relocation

**适合你，如果：**

- 愿意为长期身份、子女教育、职业天花板承担 6–18 个月周期；
- 能通过标准化大厂/中厂面试：coding + system design + behavioral；
- 家庭、伴侣职业、资产、语言环境能承受迁移。

| 地区 | 常见机制 | 求职影响 | 你要提前问的问题 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 美国 | H-1B、L-1、O-1、F-1 OPT 等 | H-1B 有抽签和时间窗口，直接海外招聘难度高 | `Do you sponsor work visas for this role? Is the role open to candidates outside the US?` |
| 加拿大 | LMIA、Global Talent Stream、省提名等 | 技术岗相对友好，但仍看公司经验 | `Can the company support relocation to Canada and timeline?` |
| 英国 | Skilled Worker visa | 需要 sponsor license 和薪资门槛 | `Is this role eligible for Skilled Worker sponsorship?` |
| 欧盟 | Blue Card、本国技术签证 | 国家差异大，德国/荷兰/爱尔兰机会较多 | `Which entity would employ me and in which country?` |
| 新加坡 | EP | 看薪资、学历、公司配额与政策 | `Is EP sponsorship available for non-local candidates?` |
| 日本 | 技术/人文知识/国际业务签证 | 英语岗少于本地语岗，但外企和 SaaS 有机会 | `Is Japanese required for engineering collaboration?` |

**90 天目标：**选 2 个主攻国家，整理 30 家明确写 sponsor/relocation 的公司，完成系统设计与行为面素材。

### 1.4 路径 C：外企在华 / 国际团队

**适合你，如果：**

- 想提升英文协作和国际项目背书，但暂时不想处理跨境税务或搬家；
- 当前简历缺少全球产品、SaaS、云原生、平台工程、AI infra 等关键词。

**打法：**

- 目标不是「外企光环」，而是拿到可迁移经历：跨时区项目、英文设计文档、全球用户规模、on-call、合规安全、SRE。
- 入职后主动争取全球项目：`I’d like to take ownership of a cross-region reliability project so I can contribute to the global roadmap.`
- 12–18 个月后，把这段经历转化成 Remote/relocation 简历 bullet。

### 1.5 路径 D：独立 contractor / fractional engineer

**适合你，如果：**

- 有明确 niche：AI app infra、LLM evals、Kubernetes cost optimization、payments、security、data platform、mobile performance；
- 能用英文写 proposal、scope、milestone、weekly update；
- 能承受 1–3 个月没有收入或客户流失。

| 模式 | 价格方式 | 适合项目 | 风险控制 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hourly | `$80–$200/hour`，视市场与稀缺性 | 调试、顾问、短期支持 | 每周 cap hours，记录 timesheet |
| Monthly retainer | `$5k–$20k/month` | 持续架构顾问、技术负责人补位 | 明确响应 SLA 和不包含事项 |
| Fixed scope | 按里程碑报价 | MVP、迁移、性能优化 | 写清验收标准和 change request |
| Fractional | 每周 1–3 天 | 初创公司缺 senior owner | 明确决策权和会议时间 |

一句话定位模板：

```text
I help B2B SaaS teams reduce cloud cost and improve reliability by redesigning Kubernetes workloads, observability, and incident processes. Typical engagements run 4–8 weeks and target 20–40% infra cost reduction or measurable SLO improvements.
```

## 2. 渠道与打法：把求职当作漏斗运营

### 2.1 漏斗指标

| 阶段 | 目标转化率 | 你每周要做的动作 | 低于目标时怎么修 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 找到匹配岗位 | 50 个/周 | 平台搜索 + 公司 careers + LinkedIn alert | 扩大国家/关键词；减少 title 限制 |
| 投递/外联 | 20–30 个/周 | 定制简历 + 找 hiring manager/recruiter | 改 headline、项目 bullet、冷信首句 |
| 回复 | 10–20% | 3–6 天 follow-up | 换人、换渠道、加具体价值 |
| recruiter screen | 5–10 个/月 | 准备薪资、签证、时区回答 | 简历 summary 与目标不匹配 |
| technical loop | 2–4 个/月 | coding/system design/behavioral | 复盘每一轮失败点 |
| offer | 1–2 个/季度 | 并行推进 | 提高申请质量和 referral 占比 |

### 2.2 We Work Remotely：找真正 remote-first 的小中型团队

网站：[We Work Remotely](https://weworkremotely.com/)

1. 进入 `Programming` 分类，关键词用 `backend`, `platform`, `infrastructure`, `AI`, `ML`, `data`, `staff`, `senior`。
2. 打开岗位先找 `Location requirements`、`Time zone overlap`、`Employment type`。
3. 只投 `Anywhere in the World`、`APAC/EMEA overlap accepted`、明确支持 contractor 的岗位。
4. 跳过只写 `Remote` 但要求 `authorized to work in the US` 的岗位，除非你已有身份。

投递备注模板：

```text
Hi team,

I’m applying for the Senior Backend Engineer role. I’ve led distributed systems work in [domain], including [specific achievement with metric]. The role stood out because you’re building [product/context from job post], and my background in [matching keyword] maps directly to the problems listed in the posting.

I’m based in China and can overlap 3–4 hours with [team timezone]. I’m open to contractor or EOR arrangements if direct employment is not available.

Best,
[Name]
```

### 2.3 RemoteOK：快速扫岗位，但要严格过滤

网站：[RemoteOK](https://remoteok.com/)

- 搜索：`senior backend`, `golang`, `rust`, `typescript`, `kubernetes`, `ai engineer`, `staff engineer`。
- 点开公司官网二次验证，RemoteOK 上的岗位可能已过期。
- 过滤掉薪资明显异常或公司信息很少的广告；优先投有清晰 JD、薪资范围、团队介绍的岗位。

快速判断清单：

- [ ] 写明 `Worldwide` 或 `Anywhere`，不是只写 `Remote`。
- [ ] 写明合同类型：employee / contractor / EOR。
- [ ] 写明薪资范围或 seniority leveling。
- [ ] JD 中有具体系统问题，而不是一堆 buzzwords。
- [ ] 公司 careers 页仍有该岗位。

### 2.4 Wellfound：适合初创公司和 contractor 机会

网站：[Wellfound](https://wellfound.com/)

**设置 profile：**

- `Open to Remote`：打开。
- `Locations`：选择 `Remote`, `Singapore`, `Canada`, `UK`, `Germany`, `Netherlands` 等目标地。
- `Role interests`：不要只写 `Software Engineer`，写 `Backend`, `Infrastructure`, `AI Engineer`, `Staff Engineer`, `Founding Engineer`。
- `Bio` 第一行直接写价值：`Senior backend/platform engineer with 9 years building high-scale SaaS systems; strong in reliability, data pipelines, and AI application infrastructure.`

给 founder 的短私信：

```text
Hi [Founder Name] — I saw [Company] is hiring a senior backend/platform engineer to work on [specific product/problem].

I’ve built [similar system] at [scale], including [metric: latency/cost/reliability/revenue]. If your current bottleneck is [inferred bottleneck], I can help design and ship the first version quickly.

Would it be useful if I sent a 3-bullet teardown of your current architecture/job requirements and how I’d approach the first 30 days?
```

### 2.5 LinkedIn：主战场不是投递按钮，而是搜索人与被搜索

Headline 不写 `Software Engineer at X`，改成关键词：

```text
Senior Backend / Platform Engineer | Distributed Systems, Kubernetes, AI Infrastructure | Remote-friendly, APAC/EMEA overlap
```

LinkedIn 内部搜索：

```text
People → Current company: [target company] → Keywords: recruiter backend remote
People → Current company: [target company] → Keywords: engineering manager platform
Posts → "we're hiring" "remote" "backend" "visa sponsorship"
```

每周 LinkedIn 动作：

1. 关注 30 家目标公司的 recruiter、EM、Staff+ engineer。
2. 评论 5 条与技术相关的招聘/工程博客帖子，评论必须有内容：`We solved a similar issue by...`。
3. 发 1 条英文短帖，展示一个具体工程判断，例如 incident 复盘、性能优化、架构 trade-off。
4. 对 10 个目标人发连接请求，3–6 天后 follow-up。

### 2.6 如何被 inbound：让 recruiter 能搜到、敢联系

Recruiter 搜索通常是关键词组合：`title + skill + location + visa/remote + seniority`。

| 方向 | 关键词 |
| --- | --- |
| Backend | `distributed systems`, `microservices`, `API design`, `PostgreSQL`, `Kafka`, `Redis`, `Go`, `Java`, `Node.js` |
| Platform/SRE | `Kubernetes`, `Terraform`, `AWS`, `GCP`, `observability`, `SLO`, `incident response`, `CI/CD` |
| AI Engineering | `LLM applications`, `RAG`, `evaluation`, `prompt engineering`, `vector databases`, `agent workflows` |
| Seniority | `technical leadership`, `mentoring`, `cross-functional`, `architecture review`, `roadmap` |
| Remote | `async communication`, `remote-first`, `APAC/EMEA overlap`, `written communication` |

LinkedIn About 模板：

```text
Senior Backend / Platform Engineer with [X]+ years of experience building reliable, high-scale systems for [domain]. I specialize in [3 skills], with recent work in [AI infra / data platform / reliability / cloud cost].

Selected impact:
- Reduced [metric] by [X%] by [action] across [scale].
- Led [project] from design to production, serving [users/requests/revenue].
- Mentored [N] engineers and drove architecture reviews across [teams].

I’m open to remote-first roles with APAC/EMEA overlap, relocation opportunities, and senior contractor engagements where clear ownership and async communication matter.
```

## 3. 简历与 LinkedIn：为海外/Remote 重写，而不是翻译中文简历

### 3.1 一页英文简历结构

```text
Name
Senior Backend / Platform Engineer
Email | LinkedIn | GitHub | Location: China | Open to Remote / Relocation | Timezone: UTC+8

SUMMARY
2 lines: seniority + domain + strongest measurable impact + remote/English collaboration.

CORE SKILLS
Languages: Go, Java, TypeScript, Python
Backend: Distributed systems, API design, PostgreSQL, Kafka, Redis
Cloud/Platform: AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform, Observability, CI/CD
AI: RAG, LLM evals, vector databases, agent workflows

EXPERIENCE
Company — Senior Software Engineer | 2021–Present
- Bullet 1
- Bullet 2
- Bullet 3

SELECTED PROJECTS / OPEN SOURCE
Education
```

### 3.2 Bullet 公式：Action + Scope + Metric + Business Outcome

不要写：

```text
Responsible for backend development and system optimization.
```

改成：

```text
Reduced p99 API latency from 1.8s to 420ms by redesigning query patterns, adding Redis caching, and removing N+1 calls, improving checkout conversion by 3.2% across 1.5M monthly users.
```

可套用模板：

```text
Built [system] from scratch using [tech], supporting [scale] and enabling [business outcome].
Reduced [bad metric] by [X%] by [technical action], saving [money/time] per [period].
Led [project/team] across [N] engineers and [M] stakeholders, delivering [result] in [time].
Migrated [legacy system] to [new architecture] with zero downtime, improving [reliability/cost/developer velocity].
Designed [architecture/API/platform] adopted by [N] teams, reducing [cycle time/incidents/manual work] by [X%].
Mentored [N] engineers through design reviews and pairing, improving [promotion/readiness/on-call quality] outcomes.
```

### 3.3 Senior/Staff 简历要突出「判断」

| 低信号写法 | 高信号写法 |
| --- | --- |
| Used Kafka for async processing | Chose Kafka over SQS after comparing ordering, replay, and consumer scaling requirements; processed 80M events/day with <0.1% retry rate |
| Improved monitoring | Defined service-level indicators and alert thresholds for checkout APIs, reducing false-positive pages by 65% |
| Led team | Led 6 engineers through a 2-quarter migration, splitting milestones into reversible steps and maintaining weekly RFC updates |

### 3.4 LinkedIn Experience 改写规则

- 每段经历 3–5 个 bullets，不要复制简历全部内容。
- 第一条写业务/用户规模，第二条写技术深度，第三条写领导力或跨团队影响。
- 加 `Skills` 到每段经历：Go、Kubernetes、Distributed Systems、AWS、System Design、Technical Leadership。
- `Featured` 放 2–3 个链接：英文简历 PDF、GitHub 项目、英文技术文章/设计文档样例。

### 3.5 ATS 与关键词匹配

每次投递前，把 JD 复制到一个文档，做三列：

| JD 关键词 | 我的证据 | 简历中出现位置 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kubernetes | 负责多集群部署、HPA、资源优化 | Skills + Experience bullet 2 |
| observability | OpenTelemetry、SLO、Grafana dashboards | Skills + Experience bullet 3 |
| async communication | RFC、PRD、weekly update | Summary + Leadership bullet |

完成标准：核心关键词 70% 以上能在简历中找到真实证据；没有证据的不要硬塞。

## 4. 面试流程差异与准备清单

典型流程：

```text
Application / Referral
→ Recruiter Screen (30 min)
→ Hiring Manager Screen (45–60 min)
→ Technical Coding or Take-home
→ System Design
→ Behavioral / Leadership
→ Team Match / Bar Raiser
→ Offer / Negotiation
```

### 4.1 Recruiter screen：目标是进入下一轮，不是把所有底牌交出去

会问：`Tell me about yourself.`、`Why this role?`、`Compensation expectation?`、`Visa sponsorship?`、`Timezone?`、`Start date?`

60 秒自我介绍：

```text
I’m a senior backend/platform engineer with [X]+ years of experience building [systems/domain]. In my recent role, I led [project] that [metric outcome], using [tech stack].

What I’m looking for now is a remote-friendly or relocation-supported role where I can own [type of problems] end to end, especially around [company-relevant area]. I’m particularly interested in [Company] because [specific product/team reason].
```

签证回答：

```text
I’m currently based in China. For this role, I’m open to three setups: remote contractor/EOR if the team supports it, relocation with visa sponsorship, or starting remotely while relocation is processed. Could you share which options are available for this position?
```

时区回答：

```text
I’m in UTC+8. I can consistently overlap [2–4] hours with [team timezone], for example [specific window]. For async work, I’m used to writing RFCs, detailed PR descriptions, and end-of-day updates so decisions don’t block on meetings.
```

### 4.2 Hiring manager screen：证明你能解决他当下的问题

准备 5 个问题：

```text
1. What are the top engineering problems this person should solve in the first 6 months?
2. Which systems are currently under the most scaling or reliability pressure?
3. How does the team make architecture decisions across time zones?
4. What does success look like for a senior engineer after 90 days?
5. Are there constraints around location, employment entity, or visa sponsorship that I should understand early?
```

回答时使用 `Context → Decision → Trade-off → Result → What I’d do differently`，不要只讲技术细节。

### 4.3 技术编码：海外面试更重视沟通

编码环节，海外面试更看重沟通与推理过程而非题量。你的现场表现要做到：

- 开始前复述题意和约束：输入范围、重复元素、排序、空值、复杂度目标。
- 先给 brute force，再优化，不要沉默 10 分钟。
- 写代码时每 2–3 分钟解释当前决策。
- 测试 3 类 case：happy path、edge case、large case。
- 结束时主动说复杂度和可改进点。

英文开场脚本：

```text
Let me first restate the problem to make sure I understand it correctly. We need to [goal], given [input], and return [output]. A few clarifying questions: [constraints].

A straightforward approach would be [brute force], which costs [complexity]. I think we can improve it by [data structure/algorithm], because [reason]. I’ll implement that and then test edge cases.
```

### 4.4 System design：资深工程师的核心筛选

系统设计准备见 [系统设计面试](03-system-design-interview.md)。本章只给求职场景的准备清单：

- 10 个常见题：URL shortener、feed、chat、notification、payment、rate limiter、metrics、search autocomplete、file storage、LLM/RAG app。
- 每题写一页模板：requirements、API、data model、high-level architecture、bottlenecks、trade-offs、failure modes。
- 每题练 45 分钟时间盒：5 分钟需求、5 分钟估算、10 分钟架构、15 分钟 deep dive、5 分钟可靠性、5 分钟 recap。
- 资深候选人必须主动讨论：multi-region、data consistency、observability、migration plan、cost。

系统设计结束 recap：

```text
To summarize, we designed [system] for [scale]. The key decisions were [decision 1] for [reason], [decision 2] for [reason], and [decision 3] to handle [failure/scale].

If we had more time, I’d go deeper on [topic], especially [trade-off]. The main risks I’d monitor in production are [risk 1], [risk 2], and [risk 3].
```

### 4.5 Behavioral：STAR 不是背故事，而是证明 seniority

准备见 [STAR 行为面试](04-behavioral-interview-star.md)。至少准备 8 个故事：

| 主题 | 必备素材 |
| --- | --- |
| Conflict | 与 PM/manager/tech lead 意见冲突，如何用数据推进 |
| Failure | 线上事故、项目延期、错误技术判断，如何修复 |
| Leadership | 无 title 影响他人，带新人，推动标准 |
| Ambiguity | 需求不清、方向变化，如何拆解并交付 |
| Cross-functional | 与产品、设计、销售、安全、法务协作 |
| Remote async | 跨时区协作、文档决策、减少会议 |
| Customer impact | 直接改善收入、留存、成本、SLA |
| Technical depth | 架构取舍、迁移、性能、可靠性 |

STAR 英文骨架：

```text
Situation: At [company/team], we were facing [problem] that affected [users/business metric].
Task: I was responsible for [ownership], and the success criteria were [metric/timeline].
Action: I did three things: first, [action]; second, [action]; third, [action]. The main trade-off was [trade-off], and I chose [decision] because [reason].
Result: We achieved [metric], and the long-term impact was [impact]. If I did it again, I would [learning].
```

### 4.6 Team match：双向筛选

问这些问题，判断团队是否适合 Remote/海外候选人：

```text
How many engineers are currently working outside the main office timezone?
What decisions are expected to be written down versus discussed in meetings?
How are incidents handled when the on-call team is distributed?
What does the team expect from a senior engineer in the first 30/60/90 days?
How often do remote engineers get promoted compared with office-based engineers?
```

如果对方回答「我们很 flexible」但没有具体机制，风险高。

## 5. 谈薪：用数据和节奏谈，不靠胆量

### 5.1 先建立薪酬锚点

数据源：

- [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/)：大厂与部分独角兽总包。
- [Glassdoor Salaries](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/index.htm)：样本多但噪音大。
- [Blind](https://www.teamblind.com/)：真实但情绪化，适合看范围。
- [H1B Salary Database](https://h1bdata.info/)：美国签证工资公开数据，只代表 base salary。
- 公司 JD 薪资区间、当地招聘法规披露区间。

锚点表：

| 字段 | 示例 |
| --- | --- |
| Company | Example SaaS Inc. |
| Level | Senior / Staff / L5 / L6 |
| Location | Remote worldwide / Canada / Germany |
| Base | `$160k` |
| Bonus | `10–15%` |
| Equity | `$80k/year vesting value` |
| Sign-on | `$20k` |
| Source | levels.fyi, recruiter range, offer post |
| Confidence | High / Medium / Low |

### 5.2 总包结构怎么读

| 组成 | 你要问清楚 | 常见坑 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Base salary | 年薪、币种、支付周期、是否本地化 | Remote worldwide 可能按地区折扣 |
| Bonus | target bonus、计算公式、是否 prorated | 不是 guaranteed cash |
| Equity/Options | RSU 还是 option、估值、行权价、税务事件 | 初创 option 可能短期不可变现 |
| Vesting | 4 年、1 年 cliff、季度/月度归属 | 离职未归属部分清零 |
| Sign-on | 分几次发、是否需返还 | 12 个月内离职可能 clawback |
| Benefits | 医疗、养老金、假期、设备、学习预算 | contractor 通常没有福利 |
| Relocation | 搬家费、临时住宿、签证律师、家属支持 | 失败后的费用责任 |

RSU 算法：

```text
Year 1 total compensation = base + target bonus + year-1 vested RSU value + sign-on
Annualized equity value = total grant value / vesting years

Base: $170,000
Bonus: 15% = $25,500
RSU grant: $240,000 over 4 years = $60,000/year
Sign-on: $30,000 year 1
Year 1 TC = 170,000 + 25,500 + 60,000 + 30,000 = $285,500
Recurring TC after sign-on = $255,500
```

### 5.3 永不先报数：用范围和反问保护上行空间

被问期望薪资时，不要直接说 `I expect $120k`。先拿 range。

Recruiter screen 脚本：

```text
I’m flexible depending on the level, location setup, and total compensation structure. Could you share the approved range for this role, including base, bonus, and equity? I’d like to make sure we’re aligned before discussing a specific number.
```

如果对方坚持：

```text
Based on my research for similar senior roles and the scope we discussed, I’d expect the overall package to be competitive with the market. I’d prefer to evaluate the full package rather than anchor on base salary too early. If helpful, I can react to the range you have approved.
```

如果系统表单必须填：

- 能填文字就写：`Market competitive; open to discussing full compensation package.`
- 必须填数字时，填你能接受的高区间低端，而不是底线。例如目标 `$180k`，可填 `$170k`，不要填 `$120k`。

### 5.4 并行多 offer：谈薪杠杆来自 BATNA

每周保持三类机会同时推进：

| 类型 | 数量 | 作用 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dream roles | 5–10 | 高上限，用于主攻 |
| Comparable roles | 10–20 | 形成市场价格和备选 offer |
| Practice roles | 5–10 | 练 recruiter screen 和 coding |

理想节奏：

```text
Week 1–2: 大量外联 + practice interviews
Week 3–4: recruiter/HM screens 并行
Week 5–6: technical loops 集中
Week 7–8: offer timing 对齐，开始谈薪
```

### 5.5 拿到口头 offer 后的竞价话术

先表达兴趣，再谈数字：

```text
Thank you — I’m excited about the offer and the team. After the conversations with [team/interviewer], I’m even more convinced that I can contribute to [specific goal].

Before I make a decision, I’d like to discuss whether there is flexibility in the compensation package. Based on the scope of the role, my experience in [domain], and market data for comparable senior roles, I was hoping to see the package closer to [target range].

Is there room to improve either the base salary, equity grant, or sign-on bonus?
```

有 competing offer，但不泄露过多：

```text
I want to be transparent that I’m in the final stages with another company, and their package is currently stronger on [base/equity/sign-on]. [Company] is still my preferred option because of [specific reason], so I’d like to see if we can close the gap.

If you could get the total package to around [number/range], I’d be comfortable moving forward quickly.
```

邮件版：

```text
Subject: Offer discussion — [Your Name]

Hi [Recruiter Name],

Thank you again for the offer. I’m genuinely excited about the opportunity to join [Company], especially the chance to work on [specific team/problem].

I reviewed the package carefully. Given the seniority of the role, my background in [relevant domain], and current market data for similar positions, I was hoping the total compensation could be closer to [target].

Would the team be able to consider one of the following adjustments?
1. Increase base salary from [current] to [target]
2. Increase equity from [current] to [target]
3. Add / increase sign-on bonus to [target]

If we can get closer to that range, I’d be ready to move forward and align on start date.

Best,
[Name]
```

要求书面确认：

```text
Thanks for confirming. Could you please send the updated offer details in writing, including base salary, bonus target, equity grant, vesting schedule, sign-on terms, relocation support, and employment entity? I’d like to review the complete package before signing.
```

### 5.6 谈 contractor rate

不要把国内月薪直接换算成 hourly。用价值、稀缺性、可用时间定价。

换算公式：

```text
Target annual income / billable weeks / billable hours per week = floor hourly rate

180,000 / 44 / 25 = $163.6/hour
```

所以报价可设：`$160–$180/hour`，或 `$12k–$18k/month retainer`。

报价脚本：

```text
For this type of engagement, my standard rate is $[X]/hour, with a weekly cap of [Y] hours. For a longer-term retainer, I can offer $[Z]/month covering [scope], [meetings], and [deliverables].

To avoid surprises, I suggest we define the first milestone as [specific deliverable] and review progress after two weeks.
```

## 6. 时区、异步协作与英语沟通

Remote 面试会隐性评估：你是否会让跨时区团队变慢。

| 团队 | 北京时间可 overlap | 备注 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| US West | 22:00–01:00 或 07:00–09:00 | 长期熬夜不可持续，优先谈固定会议日 |
| US East | 20:00–23:00 | 比西海岸稍好 |
| UK | 16:00–19:00 / 17:00–20:00 | 适合中国远程 |
| Central Europe | 15:00–18:00 / 16:00–19:00 | 适合中国远程 |
| Singapore/Australia | 白天或接近白天 | 最友好 |

面试中说明你的工作方式：

```text
My default remote workflow is: write the context before the meeting, make decisions visible in a doc or issue, and send an end-of-day update with blockers and next steps. I try to reserve overlap time for high-bandwidth discussions rather than status updates.
```

Daily update：

```text
Yesterday:
- Shipped [PR/link] for [feature/fix].
- Investigated [issue] and found [root cause].

Today:
- Finish [task] and request review by [time].
- Start [next task] after [dependency].

Blockers / decisions needed:
- Need input on [decision]. Options: A [trade-off], B [trade-off]. My recommendation is [choice] because [reason].
```

PR 描述：

```text
## What changed
[Concise summary]

## Why
[Problem, user impact, metric]

## How
[Key implementation decisions]

## Trade-offs
[What you considered and why you chose this]

## Test plan
- [ ] Unit tests: [command/result]
- [ ] Integration/manual: [steps]
- [ ] Rollback: [how]
```

更多英文写作训练见 [技术写作英语](../english/03-technical-writing-english.md)。

## 7. 完整实操示例：30 天启动海外 Remote 求职

### 第 1 周：定位与资产

2. 写一句定位：

```text

```

3. 完成英文简历 V1：至少 8 个 metric bullets。
4. LinkedIn 改 headline、about、experience、featured。
5. 建表：

| Company | Role | Path | Timezone | Visa/Remote | Contact | Source | Status | Next action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

### 第 2 周：渠道启动

1. We Work Remotely 找 15 个岗位。

5. 发出 20 封外联，记录每封的首句和状态。

完成标准：至少 3 个回复；如果低于 10%，改首句和匹配证据。

### 第 3 周：面试准备与复盘

1. 每天 1 道 coding，重点练边写边口述（先复述题意与约束，再讲思路，最后写）。
2. 每两天 1 道 system design，按 [系统设计面试](03-system-design-interview.md) 时间盒。
3. 写 8 个 STAR 故事，按 [STAR 行为面试](04-behavioral-interview-star.md) 录音。
4. 每次 recruiter screen 后写复盘：

```text
Company:
Role:
Questions asked:
Where I was clear:
Where I rambled:
Compensation/visa/timezone info:
Next improvement:
```

### 第 4 周：提高质量与谈薪准备

1. 对回复过但未推进的人发 follow-up。
2. 为 5 个最匹配岗位写 `30-day plan`：

```text
First 30 days:
- Understand [system/team metric] by reviewing [docs/dashboards/code].
- Ship a small but valuable improvement: [specific likely task].
- Identify the top 2 reliability/cost/developer velocity bottlenecks.
- Write an RFC for [larger initiative] with trade-offs and rollout plan.
```

3. 建薪酬锚点表，至少 10 条数据。
4. 写好三版谈薪目标：最低可接受、满意、理想。

## 8. 常见坑 & 排查表

| 症状 | 可能原因 | 排查动作 | 修复方法 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 投了 50 个岗位 0 回复 | 岗位不支持所在地；简历关键词不匹配；没有 referral | 抽样 10 个 JD，看 location/entity；做关键词表 | 只投 worldwide/APAC/EMEA；重写 summary；找 HM 外联 |
| recruiter 一问薪资就结束 | 数字过低显得不 senior，或过高但无证据 | 记录对方 range 和你的回答 | 先问 range；准备 levels.fyi 数据；谈 total comp |
| HM 面后无反馈 | 只讲职责，没有讲决策和影响 | 复盘回答是否包含 trade-off/metric | 用 Context→Decision→Trade-off→Result 重写故事 |
| coding 过不了 | 英文沉默、边界条件漏、复杂度讲不清 | 录屏/录音一次 mock | 每题固定开场、clarifying questions、test cases |
| system design 被评价不够 senior | 只画组件，没有讨论瓶颈/可靠性/成本 | 看是否主动估算 QPS/storage | 加 capacity、failure modes、migration、observability |
| Remote 面试被拒 | 时区回答不具体；没有异步证据 | 检查 LinkedIn/About 是否写 remote workflow | 准备 overlap window 和 PR/RFC 样例 |
| offer 数字低 | 过早报底线；没有并行流程 | 查看第一次薪资回答 | 延后报数；每周保持 comparable roles |
| contractor 客户拖 scope | 没有里程碑和 change request | 查看合同/邮件是否有验收标准 | 固定 scope、每周 cap、变更另报价 |
| relocation 卡住 | 公司不 sponsor 或岗位国家不匹配 | 第一次 screen 是否确认 sponsor | 早问 sponsorship；优先有 sponsor 历史的公司 |
| 入职后痛苦 | remote-first 口号但会议驱动 | 面试没问决策机制 | team match 阶段问文档、晋升、on-call、跨时区比例 |

## 11. 合规、税务与风险边界

本文不构成法律、税务、移民建议。真正开始跨境收款、签约、搬迁前，至少确认：

- **税务居民身份**：你在哪个国家/地区纳税，是否存在双重征税协定。
- **合同主体**：个人、个体户、公司、EOR、当地雇佣实体分别承担什么义务。
- **发票与收款**：客户需要 invoice、W-8BEN/W-8BEN-E、VAT/GST 信息时你如何提供。
- **知识产权**：合同是否约定工作成果归属、开源贡献、竞业限制。
- **数据与安全**：是否处理生产数据、个人信息、跨境数据传输。
- **签证限制**：旅游签证通常不能工作；学生/配偶签证也可能有工时限制。

给律师/税务顾问的问题清单：

```text
I will be based in [country/region] and provide software engineering services to a company in [country].
The expected annual income is [amount], paid via [platform/bank/wire].
The contract type is [employee/contractor/company-to-company/EOR].

Questions:
1. Where will this income be taxable?
2. What registration or invoicing setup do I need?
3. Are there withholding tax or reporting requirements?
4. What contract clauses should I review carefully?
5. Does this work affect my current or future visa status?
```
